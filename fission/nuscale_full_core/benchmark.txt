McSAFER NuScale-like SMR Full-Core Neutronics Benchmark
========================================================

Paper:    "Neutronics Benchmark of the NuScale-like SMR in the McSAFER Project"
Authors:  J. Bousquet et al.
Journal:  Journal of Nuclear Engineering, 2025, 6(4), 44
URL:      https://www.mdpi.com/2673-4362/6/4/44

Project:  McSAFER (Multi-Criteria Analysis of Safety, Environmental, and
          Financial aspects of Energy systems based on Renewable sources
          and small modular Reactors)
Funding:  EU Horizon 2020 (Grant Agreement No. 945063)

Description
-----------
This benchmark defines a NuScale-like Small Modular Reactor (SMR) full core
for code-to-code comparison of neutronics calculations. The NuScale Power
Module is a 77 MWe (160-250 MWth) integral pressurized water reactor
designed for factory fabrication and modular deployment.

This is the FULL CORE model with all 37 fuel assemblies, as opposed to the
single-assembly infinite-lattice model in ../nuscale_smr/.

Key Design Features
-------------------
- 37 fuel assemblies in a 7x7 grid (corners removed)
  -- smallest full-core reactor benchmark
- 17x17 Westinghouse-type fuel assemblies
- 3 enrichment zones: 1.6%, 2.4%, 3.1% wt U-235
- Short active fuel length: 200 cm (vs ~366 cm in a standard PWR)
- Natural circulation primary cooling (no reactor coolant pumps)
- BORON-FREE OPERATION: no dissolved boric acid in the moderator
- Core barrel (SS304) with water downcomer

Core Loading Pattern
--------------------
    Row 0:   _    _    3    3    3    _    _
    Row 1:   _    3    2    2    2    3    _
    Row 2:   3    2    1    1    1    2    3
    Row 3:   3    2    1    1    1    2    3
    Row 4:   3    2    1    1    1    2    3
    Row 5:   _    3    2    2    2    3    _
    Row 6:   _    _    3    3    3    _    _

    1 = 1.6 wt% U-235 (9 assemblies, center)
    2 = 2.4 wt% U-235 (12 assemblies, middle ring)
    3 = 3.1 wt% U-235 (16 assemblies, outer ring)
    _ = water (no assembly)

Simplifications in This Model
------------------------------
- Gd2O3 burnable absorber pins are omitted (the original benchmark has
  7 assembly types with varying Gd content)
- Only 3 assembly types differentiated by enrichment
- HZP conditions (fuel and moderator at same temperature)
- 2D model with reflective axial BCs (no axial reflector modeled)

Reference Solution
------------------
Code:     Serpent 2 / SCALE KENO-VI (continuous-energy Monte Carlo)
Library:  ENDF/B-VII.1, JEFF-3.3, ENDF/B-VIII.0 (varies by participant)
k-eff:    ~1.01-1.03 (HZP, ARO, fresh fuel, full benchmark spec)
          Note: without Gd2O3, k-eff will be somewhat higher

Multiple participants calculated this benchmark:
  - Serpent 2 (VTT, reference solution)
  - OpenMC
  - MCNP
  - SCALE/KENO-VI (agrees within 44 pcm of Serpent)
  - MORET
  - Various deterministic codes

Reported code-to-code differences are on the order of 44-500 pcm.
