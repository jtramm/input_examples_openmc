VVER-1000 Mock-up Benchmark
============================

Full Name:
    VVER-1000 Mock-up Criticality Experiments at the LR-0 Research Reactor

Reference:
    F. Setiawan, M. Lemaire, D. Lee,
    "Analysis of VVER-1000 mock-up criticality experiments with nuclear data
    library ENDF/B-VIII.0 and Monte Carlo code MCS,"
    Nuclear Engineering and Technology, Vol. 53, pp. 1-18, 2021.
    DOI: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.net.2020.06.015

Additional References:
    - IRPhE benchmark documentation (detailed geometry/material data)
    - SINBAD database NEA-1517/82 (experimental measurements)

Facility:
    LR-0 research reactor, Research Center Rez, Czech Republic
    Light-water-moderated zero-power reactor, max continuous power 1 kW

Experiment Description:
    The VVER-1000 mock-up reproduces a sector of a typical VVER-1000 reactor
    core. It consists of 32 dismountable hexagonal fuel assemblies arranged
    with 23.6 cm pitch. Each standard assembly contains 312 UO2 fuel pins,
    18 B4C absorber guide tubes, and 1 central instrumentation tube, arranged
    in a triangular lattice with 12.75 mm pin pitch.

    Three UO2 enrichments are used: 2.0%, 3.0%, and 3.3% U-235.

    Six critical configurations were achieved by varying the moderator level
    (51.3 to 150.0 cm) and boric acid concentration (2.85 to 4.68 g/kg H3BO3).
    Four radial pin power maps were also measured.

    Experiments were conducted at room temperature and atmospheric pressure.

Reference Results (MCS with ENDF/B-VII.1, Table 3 of paper):
    Case 1: keff = 1.00137 (+137 pcm vs experiment)
    Case 2: keff = 1.00307 (+307 pcm vs experiment)
    Case 3: keff = 1.00403 (+403 pcm vs experiment)
    Case 4: keff = 1.00462 (+462 pcm vs experiment)
    Case 5: keff = 1.00495 (+495 pcm vs experiment)
    Case 6: keff = 1.00532 (+532 pcm vs experiment)

Reference Results (MCS with ENDF/B-VIII.0, Table 4 of paper):
    Case 1: keff = 1.00153 (+153 pcm vs experiment)
    Case 2: keff = 1.00321 (+321 pcm vs experiment)
    Case 3: keff = 1.00429 (+429 pcm vs experiment)
    Case 4: keff = 1.00483 (+483 pcm vs experiment)
    Case 5: keff = 1.00523 (+523 pcm vs experiment)
    Case 6: keff = 1.00559 (+559 pcm vs experiment)

Technological Uncertainty (3 sigma):
    Total: 300 to 468 pcm depending on case
    Major contributors: boric acid concentration, fuel enrichment,
    fuel assembly pitch, cladding thickness

This Model:
    Single hexagonal fuel assembly, 2D infinite lattice with reflective BCs.
    Uses openmc.HexLattice with 11 rings (331 positions).
    Demonstrates VVER-type hexagonal lattice construction in OpenMC.
